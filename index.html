How to play

  • Press Start. A new word appears every second.
  • Type a word and hit Enter/Pop to pop it.
  • Score = word length; wrong entry subtracts the characters you typed.
  • Streak counts consecutive correct pops; track Words/Score/Streak in the stats bubble.
  • Levels stack features: 1 static words; 2 moving words; 3 floating HUD; 4 adds bounce rotation; 5 swaps themes every second; 6 swaps themes at hyper speed.
  • Pick a theme (Dark, Light, Neon, Citrus glow, Retro CRT, Mint sky) anytime.

Best on a computer

It looks like you're on a phone-sized screen. For the smoothest play, open this on a larger display.
